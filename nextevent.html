Baisakhi 2010 organized by Pujari

  • April 17th, 2010 (Saturday)
    at Berkmar High School,
    405 Pleasant Hill Rd. NW, Lilburn, GA 30047


Pujari will celebrate Baisakhi on April 17th, 2010 at Berkmar High School between 3:00pm to 9:00pm. Come join us to celebrate the harvest festival of Baisakhi with us and amongst friends. Enjoy a day filled with fun, music and dance.

This year we have invited renouned artist Aakash Deep from Kolkata, to perform here on our stage. You can read all about him on his website. You can also catch a glimpse of his performance below.
