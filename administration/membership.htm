Membership
Pujari is a member's organization and is committed to serving its members. In the annual general body meeting, the members decide Pujari's regular programs for the whole year and fix the dates. This general body meeting also fixes the yearly membership fee. The yearly membership fee is roughly the aggregate of suggested donations of all programs hosted by Pujari in that year.

Pujari traditionally hosts
- Saraswati Puja,
- Baisakhi,
- Picnic & Sports,
- Durga Puja and
- Laxmi Puja.
It also hosts periodic conferences and meetings to allow members to network, learn and share experiences for personal and career development.

Besides all these, Pujari also raises funds for victims and poor, hosts cultural and entertaining programs.

What are the advantages of being a Pujari member?

  • Pujari is a member's organization and is committed to serving its members.
  • Being a member gives you a sense of belonging and ownership. Pujari is proud to be a member's organization.
  • You can elect, nominate or be elected in the Pujari's board and executive committee.
  • Your membership fee is Tax Deductible.
  • You get special discounted tickets for all our special events.
  • You get free entry and food in regular ceremonies like Durga Puja, Saraswati Puja, Laxmi Puja, Baisakhi and Picnic.
  • Join in special events for children and youth.

Overall, being a member more than pays off for all the expenditures you would normally do when you attend Pujari functions, plus you get the benefit of tax deduction and preferred seating.

How does Pujari manage all its events with such a nominal membership fee?

Thanks to our Sponsors and Donors for their generous donation and to our committed members we are able to plan and manage our finances.

Members of the Board of Directors and Executive Committee members help tremendously by providing their personal assets and money. These expenses they incur are considered as donation to Pujari.
