Annual report for the year 2002

Year 2002 was an excellent year for Pujari. We have accomplished a plenty of things, streamlined most of the regular activities. Here are the major achievements.

  1. Received the advance ruling of tax-exemption from the federal government.
  2. Has been approved for Nonprofit Standard Mail rate from the United States Postal Services.
  3. Website’s hit rate has increased and it has placed itself as a portal of the Bengali community of Georgia.
  4. Started a news group and today its members’ size is 163.
  5. Bought a complete sound system for hosting small cultural program which is an important activity of Pujari.
  6. Celebrated Durga puja, Laxmi puja and Saraswati puja at IACA.
  7. Organized two other programs: Bengali New Years and Rabindra Jayanti with Atlanta Bengali Forum and Annual Picnic with Bengali Association of Greater Atlanta. Both have been huge success and they help building bridges with other Bengali organizations. Pujari has a constant interest to host such joint programs in future.
  8. Co-hosted Health Fair with Telegu Association of Metro Atlanta and other organizations in the global mall.
  9. Organized excellent cultural programs to promote the Bengali culture in the Durga Puja, Saraswati puja and Bengali New Years – Rabindra Jayanti Festival.
  10. Conducted varieties of competitions for kids and teens to promote the Bengali culture.
  11. A lot of new members have joined in the Pujari in this year.
  12. Published an excellent puja brochure.
  13. Participated in election of IACA and continued to be a respected member in the board of IACA.
  14. Maintained a good relationship with other similar organizations such as BAGA, SEBA and ABF.
  15. Working closely with other nonprofit organizations like SEBA, IPN, IACA, FIA etc for any good cause. IPN and IACA have shown interest to organize any event jointly.
