Pujari

Pujari is a nonprofit organization based in Atlanta, promoting the diverse cultural heritage of India in general and the varied culture of Bengal in particular. Pujari is one of the oldest organizations in Georgia and continues to organize some of the prominent Bengali festivals like Durga Puja and other events at various times of the year. Members of all communities participate in the festivities and many of them play active roles in the cultural programs, which include songs, dances, elocution and drama.

Over the years, Pujari has built up a large band of patrons and has a wide reach, particularly among the Bengali community of Georgia and neighboring states.
