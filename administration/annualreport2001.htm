Annual report for the year 2001

Year 2001 was a great year for Pujari. Here are the major achievements.

  1. Pujari has been registered as a non-profit organization in the state of Georgia.
  2. Formed the initial board of directors and executive committee to carry on the official and regular activities.
  3. Established a bank account of the organization in the name of “Pujari Inc” in SouthTrust Bank.
  4. The executive committee has applied for tax-exemption status from IRS.
  5. Pujari has started and running a website.
  6. Maintain the database of attendees with correct address, phone and email.
  7. The executive committee has bought the idol of Durga.
  8. Moved all donated assets to Subhojit Roy and Kanti Das’ house.
  9. Bought light sets for stage lighting that is generally needed in the festival time and built switchboards that can be used in the stage of performing arts.
  10. Celebrated Durga puja at IACA.
  11. Made a decoration structure, that can be used in decorating stage of performing arts or festival. It can be assembled on the fly in many different ways as needed, has a great reusability.
  12. Organized an excellent cultural program to promote the Bengali culture in the Durga Puja Festival.
  13. Conducted varieties of competitions for kids, teens and ladies.
  14. Published an excellent puja brochure in book format and a web version.
  15. Helped other nonprofit organizations to sell their products and do publicity in our festival.
  16. Participated in fund raising program organized by South Eastern Bengali Association (SEBA).
  17. Participated in festival of India, organized by Indian American Cultural Association (IACA).
  18. Participated in election of IACA and continued to a respected member in the BOD of IACA.
  19. Maintained a good relationship with other similar organizations such as BAGA, SEBA and ABF.
  20. Working closely with other nonprofit organizations like SEBA, IPN, IACA, FIA etc. IPN and IACA have shown interest to organize any event jointly.
