Request for Donations to Buy new Durga Protima

Sun Apr 10, 2005
Atlanta, Georgia 

Dear Patron: 

It’s that time of year when we start planning for our most important annual event, Durga Puja. Even though the event lasts just a couple of days, it takes careful planning and commitment in terms of time, money and effort to ensure a smooth presentation. Our volunteers work hard to make it successful.

At Pujari, as you may well know, we have been very meticulous, over the years, in strictly adhering to the customs and traditions associated with Durga Puja. What many of you may not know is the kind of dogged perseverance it has taken us over the course of the last few years to maintain the appearance of the images of Goddess Durga and the other deities. With each passing year, it has become more and more difficult to do Puja given to the gradual degradation of the idols. Despite our best efforts, some of the damages to the deities are now beyond repair. 

In view of these facts, the Pujari executive committee and BOD has decided to obtain a new set of images from Calcutta , starting with this year’s Durga Puja. As you may understand, this is obviously a huge effort and requires tremendous coordination, involvement and money. As our patron, we are counting on you for your donation in support of this good and noble cause. As the whole process of ordering and obtaining the images is estimated to take more than four months, we have already started to collect donations. Please donate generously to make this initiative a grand success. As always, we appreciate your supports.

Please send your donations to:
Sushanta Saha, Treasurer of Pujari, 220 Ashlee Oaks Ct. , Alpharetta , GA 30022 .
