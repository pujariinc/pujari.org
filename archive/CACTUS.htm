CACTUS                    

                      

W h o      a r e       w e? !

A band, if you like things simple.

A Bangla rock band.

Surprised??

 

Bengal prides itself on it’s past…

(1861-1941) > Rabindranath Tagore

(1921-1992)> Satyajit Ray

(1910-1997) > Mother Teresa

So what’s modern Bangla really like?

Now that’s a tale we’d like to tell.

Plug into our music and you’ll find the mix ‘n’ match of cultures that define Banglaa today. The cosmo-banglaa sound. That’s who we are….

 

 

MEMBERS:

 

Siddhartha:  If medicine taught him the human anatomy, music taught him the ways of the mind. For Dr. Siddhartha Sankar Ray every song is a personal journey. A journey where he shares his love, hate, laughter and anger with every face in the crowd.

 

 

  Sibaji: The backbone of the band. He does a double role for the band… gets us started and holds us together when the rest of us fly…the drummer boy!

 

 

Sandip: The Creator was going through one of his mood swings… and Sandip was born… he provides the groove with his flamboyant bass playing… a rock head…

 

 

Sayak: The singing machine who loves to ride the highs…

 

                                                                                                          

Allan: Quiet and unassuming, Allan loves being part of a rock n' roll band and believes that is the only way to get high!

 

Sudipto: He is a bagful of surprise… believes in rock till u drop… Ready to set the keys on fire…
