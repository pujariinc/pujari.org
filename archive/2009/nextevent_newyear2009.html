New Year's Party 2009

organized by Pujari

  • December 31st, 2009 (Thursday) at Bombay Grill Restaurant,
    2165 Savoy Dr, Chamblee, GA 30341

Thanks to many of you we have SOLD OUT all tickets set aside with promotional pricing ($35 adults, $18 kids).

Few more tickets are left at normal pricing now ($40 adults, $20 kids). You can continue to buy them online from Sulekha. We will also be selling them on a first come first serve basis at Bombay Grill tomorrow Dec 31st from 11am to 12.30pm (ask for a Pujari rep). You may want to avail of the later option to avoid Sulekha service charges.


Pujari will host the New Year Bash 2009 on Dec. 31st, 2009 at Bombay Grill Restaurant. The celebration will include live music by a DJ who will entertain everyone by playing Bollywood hits throughout the night. There will be lots of fun, sumptuous food, cash bar, dance competitions and prizes, free champagne at midnight and fireworks display. Come join us for the fun and welcome the new year.

The menu for the dinner will include:
Appetizers

Veg. Samosa, Ragra Patties, Chilli Paneer fry, Seekh Kabab, Hariyali Kabab, Chicken 65.

Main Course

Jeera Rice, Naan/Kulcha, Salad/Raita/Pickles, Vegetable Jalfarezi, Kadhai Paneer, Tava Veg., Garlic Catfish, Kadhai Gosht, Chicken Kali Mirch.

Desert

Vanilla Ice cream, Gajar ka Halwa, Fresh fruits.
