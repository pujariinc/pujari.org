Anjali Mantra

Purpose:

One offers flowers and extreme devotion to Devi Durga.  Also, as a commoner one asks for his or her common wishes to enjoy life.

Puspanjali:

O'n Aurdehi rupong dehi bhagyan dehi bhagawati dehi me
Putran dehi dhawan dehi sarban kamanswa dehi me
Esho sa chandana hring o'n bhagabwati durgaoi nama

O'n  haro papam haro kleshang haro shokong
haro rogong haro khovong haro marim haro prye
Esho sachandana puspanjali hring on bhagabwati durgaoi nama

O'n kayeno manasa bacha karmana yat kritang maya
gwanagwan kritong papang durge twang haro durgatim
esho sachandano puspanjali hring on bhagabwati durgaoi nama

Pranam mantra:

O'n sarba mangalamangalle shive swarbartha swadhike
swaranwe trwambake gouri narawani nomohostute
