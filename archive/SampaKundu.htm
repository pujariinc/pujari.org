Melody- An Evening with Sampa

Oct 5, 2008 presented by Pujari , Atlanta

“..voice that made the people sit up during Pujas, was that of Shampa Kundu’s….Shampa is naturally on a winning streak “ – The Sunday Statesman, Nov 3, 1996

Sampa Kundu is a leading name in the field of singing. She has started her career of singing at a very early age and has proven her musical expertise not only in India but abroad too. She has performed in various shows in North America's New Jersey, New York, Boston, Washington, Dallas etc. She is representing the 21st century singer, and is bestowed with prestigious awards by the global institutions for her outstanding performance and contribution to the arena of music.

She completed her MA (Vocal music) from Rabindra Bharati University in 1992. She has got her training from her father Late Subir Kundu (disciple of Late Tarapada Kundu). She got her classical training from Shri Amalendu Pal (Disciple of Ustad Bade Ghulam Ali Khan) and above all, she has learnt light classical and light music from Sri Sukumar Mitra, Shri Anal Chatterjee and Shri Jotilaswar Mukherjee and Shri Amal Pal.This Bengali singer just loves the fact that she has taken up her career as a singer. Listening to music happenes to be her favourite hobby. After making a name for herself in the music arena, the talented singer is now trying to create something of her own. She has created her own singing group and sings songs for her own album. At the same time she is associated with a musical organization called "Sampriti".
