Editorial

Art, literature, music and dance are the main parts of Bengali culture. There is almost no Bengali who didn't think about writing a poem at his teen age. Literature has gone so much into the Bengali culture. This spirit drives us to publish a puja brochure every year.

We are collecting the material for Pujari's annual brochure which will be published in the festival of Durga puja. The brochure is distributed free of cost at the festival of Durga puja.
