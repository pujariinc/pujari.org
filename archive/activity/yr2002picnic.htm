Picnic 2002
In an unprecedented move BAGA and PUJARI have decided to organize a PICNIC together!
Thank you for joining the party with your family and friends and making this joint endeavor a great success.
We had lots of fun-filled activities for adults as well as for kids.
Registration Fee:
Adults $10.00 per person
Children (K-12) $5.00 per child.
Pre-schoolers and smaller children are free.
College-going children will be charged at adult rate.
Venue: EAST ROSWELL PARK
9000 Fouts Road, Roswell, Georgia
Phone: 770-594-6134
Date: Saturday, August 31, 2002
Time: From 11:00 AM - 5:00PM

DIRECTIONS

From I-85:
Take Exit 99 (Jimmy Carter Blvd.)
Make RIGHT at the ramp if coming from I-85 (S).
Make a LEFT at the ramp if coming from I-85 (N).
After about 5 miles Jimmy Carter Blvd., becomes Holcomb Bridge Road.
Cross Spalding Dr. and Nesbit Ferry intersections.
After the Nesbit Ferry intersection bear to the left. In approximately 1.5 miles, make a LEFT onto FOUTS ROAD, which is across KOHL's on your right. 

From GA 400:
Take Exit 7 and make a LEFT at the ramp if coming from GA 400(S).
Take Exit 7A if coming from GA 400(N).
In about 2 miles you will see a Texaco and KROGER mall to your left. Just past the mall, at the next light make a RIGHT onto FOUTS ROAD, which is across KOHL's on your left.

Once on FOUTS ROAD, go all the way past a STOP sign till the road ends. Make a RIGHT onto A. C. Lavender Dr. Drive past a STOP sign between the baseball/soccer fields to the Main Building on your RIGHT. The shelter is past the Main Building to your RIGHT across the tennis courts.

From I-285:
Take Exit 27 for GA 400 (N) then follow the directions above.
