On October 8th, as part of its grand Durga Puja celebrations, Pujari cordially invites you to the India American Cultural Association (IACA) auditorium in Smyrna for an extraordinary dance program by the famed dancer Sutapa Talukdar and her troupe.

 

Sutapa Talukdar (formerly Sutapa DattaGupta) is one of the front ranking performers of Odissi, acclaimed by critics and connoisseurs all over the world. Her performance reminds one of the grace and beauty of temple sculptures, while its lyrical qualities are akin to a poem. A disciple of the great dancer and dance teacher Guru Kelucharan Mohapatra, she has performed extensively in both India and abroad. She is the founder of the unique dance school, “Gurukul”, which was inaugurated in 1987 by Guru Kelucharan Mahapatra and actress-director-writer Aparna Sen. As its name signifies, Gurukul tries to impart knowledge on the lines of the ancient gurukul system.

 

Sutapa and her troupe bring to Atlanta her new composition “Amazing Grace”, featuring unique Yogic and Tantric elements of the Odissi dance form.

 

Come join us on October 8th at the IACA auditorium for an evening of unparalleled beauty! Experience the sculptures of the Sun Temple at Konarak, brought to life by Sutapa Talukdar and her disciples.

 

Pujari, the Atlanta organization known for its track record of bringing to Atlanta musicians and performers of the highest order, has done it again! Enjoy a rare evening of beauty and class, put forth by one of the living legends of the lyrical Odissi dance form, Sutapa Talukdar!!
