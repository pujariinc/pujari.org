Baisakhi 2003

Sabare Kori Aaobhan

Pujari and Atlanta Bengali Forum cordially invite you to Baishakhi (Nababarsha) - a grand cultural evening from Bengali community to celebrate the Bengali New Year. In this occasion, we will have performances from formidable local talents of Atlanta. Please mark your calendar and join us in paying homage to Kabiguru Rabindranath, Kazi Nazrul and the others from Bengal.

To make it convenient for you, we have made arrangements for dinner. Please mark your calendars and make it a great success.
