Picnic 2005

Pujari picnic is synonymous with great food and gathering of fun-loving people. Bring your family and friends and make this year's picnic a success.
Activities such as musical chair, three-legged race, cricket with soft ball and fun children activities will surely make it a fun event. To sample how we did last year,  you may click here.
This year we will have it at  Upper Overlook Park Shelter# 302 , located on the south bank of Lake Lanier and the east side of Buford Dam in Gwinnett County.

Registration Fee:
Adults $10.00 per person
Children (K-12) $5.00 per child.
- Pre-schoolers and smaller children are free.
- Pujari members and their family persons are already registered and this event is no fee for them.
Venue: Lake Lanier (Buford Dam) 
Shelter# 302 Upper Overlook Park
Date: Saturday, May 21, 2005
Time: From 11:00 AM - 5:00PM

Direction:

From down town Atlanta :
- Drive along I-85 north and exit at Exit 113 (at left side of I-85) for I-985 north
- Turn to Rd 20 at exit 4 of I-985, toward Lake Lanier
- Drive about 7 miles on Rd 20, which changes its name from Nelson Brogdon Blvd to Cumming Hwy,
  and turn right to Suwanee Dam Rd
- At the end of Suwanee Dam Rd, turn left to Buford Dam Rd.
- Upper Overlook Park is on your left side (less than one mile).

 From Duluth:
- Drive along Buford Hwy or Peachtree Industry Blvd north
-  Turn left to Lawrenceville-Suwanee Dam Rd north. At the end of the road (8-10 miles) is Buford Dam Rd.
- Turn left Buford Dam Rd and drive less than one mile. You will see Upper Overlook Park on your left side.

Once on Upper Overlook Park you need to find picnic shelter#302.
