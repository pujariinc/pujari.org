Fancy Dress Competetion

FancyDress Competition will be held on Sunday, 17th October, on the second day of the Durga Puja celebrations There will be two groups for this competition:

  • Group A. Till the age of 5 years
  •  Group B. From 6 years to 10 years.

The theme of the competition will be Aamra Bharotiyo (People of India). This means they can dress up based on any Indian characters, be it our National Leaders like, Netaji or Indira Gandhi or a character from the streets of India, like a tea seller or a rickshaw puller. Also one other criteria is that they will have to enact something related to the characters so as to emphasise their particular trait.

There will be prizes for the winners in each group. For participation and more details please contact any of the following:
