Picnic 2004
Don't miss the party.  This is one the few rare occasions that Atlanta Bengali people miss.
Great food, excellent company, tons of games and probably you will solve world's all kind of problems as you always do in any party. Fun, fun fun.
Bring your family and friends and make this joint endeavor a great success.
We will have a lots of fun-filled activities for adults as well as for kids. There will be cricket for cricket lovers
(tennis for individuals for whom cricket is not appealing ),
musical chair and different children activities.
Registration Fee:
Adults $10.00 per person
Children (K-12) $5.00 per child.
Pre-schoolers and smaller children are free.
College-going children will be charged at adult rate.
* * * Pujari members and their family persons are already registered and this event is no fee for them. * * *
Venue: East Roswell Park
9000 Fouts Road
Roswell, GA 30075

Direction:

From GA 400:
Take Exit 7 and make a LEFT at the ramp if traveling on GA 400(S).
Take Exit 7A if traveling on GA 400(N).
In about 2 miles you will see a Texaco and KROGER mall to your left. Just past the mall, at the next light make a RIGHT onto FOUTS ROAD, which is across Eckerd's/KOHL's on your left.

From I-285:
Take Exit 27 for GA 400 (N) then follow the directions above.

From I-85:
Take Exit 99 (Jimmy Carter Blvd.)
Make RIGHT at the ramp if traveling on I-85 (S).
Make a LEFT at the ramp if traveling on I-85 (N).
After about 5 miles Jimmy Carter Blvd., becomes Holcomb Bridge Road.
Cross Spalding Dr. and Nesbit Ferry intersections.
After the Nesbit Ferry intersection bear to the left. In approximately 1.5 miles, make a LEFT onto FOUTS ROAD, which is across Eckerd's/KOHL's on your right.

Once on FOUTS ROAD , go all the way past a STOP sign till the road ends. Make a RIGHT onto A. C. Lavender Dr. Drive past a STOP sign between the baseball/soccer fields to the Main Building on your RIGHT. The Community Room 101 is in the main building. The PICNIC Shelter is next to the fountain near the tennis courts.
