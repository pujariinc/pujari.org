Appetiser Party at Dunwoody
 
We are organizing an informal social get-together at Dunwoody Public Library.
It is an opportunity to taste some delicious appetizer and enjoy some top-end musical notes.
 
Roma Mondal, famous Rabindra Sangeet exponent from Kolkata will present songs at this evening. Her profile can be seen at http://www.romamondal.com/prof.htm.
 
Thanks to generous donations from some of our community members such as Anjan and Indrani Dutta-Gupta, Pranab Lahiri, PK and Shyamoli Das we will not have any entry ticket. However we encourage you to donate at your will to wncourage and support visiting artists like her.

Venue and Time: Dunwoody Public Library Williams room at 7:30 to 11:00 pm.
