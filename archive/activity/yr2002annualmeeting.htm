Annual meeting 2002

Yearly annual meeting was held on December 14 2002, Saturday, from 3 pm to 5 pm.

Venue: Mountain View Library.
3320 Sandy Plains Road, Marietta, GA 30066.
Ph: 770-509-2725.

Direction

Take Interstate 75 north to Exit  263 (formerly exit 112) – South Marietta Parkway. Turn right onto the South Marietta Parkway. Go through two traffic lights and exit north onto Hwy. 120 – Roswell Road. Turn right onto Roswell Road and travel approximately one mile. Turn left onto East Piedmont Road. At the first traffic light , turn right onto Sewell Mill Road. Turn left onto Holly Springs Road. Continue along Holly Springs Road until it dead ends. Turn right onto Sandy Plains Road. The library will be immediately to your right.

Agenda

  1. General annual report by the President.
  2. Financial report by the Treasurer.
  3. Amend bylaws.
  4. Election of the Bard of Directors.
  5. Formation of Executive Committee.
  6. Fix membership fee for 2003.
  7. Publish the events of 2003 and dates.
  8. Planning of Saraswati Puja
