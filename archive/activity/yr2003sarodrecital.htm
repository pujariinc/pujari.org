Debojyoti Bose naturally inherits an unstained musical acumen, for he was born in a family of passionate musicians at Kolkata - the citadel of Indian Classical Music. His father Pandit Biswanath Bose, disciple of Late Pandit Kanthe Maharaj of Benaras and his mother Smt. Bharati Bose, a keen Sitar player and a disciple of Mian Davir Khan, Ustad Mustaq Ali Khan and Ustad Ali Akbar Khan, sowed the seed of music in him by imparting the early lessons. Debojyoti has received talim in Sarod for over twenty years from Ustad Amjad Ali Khan of Senia Banghas Gharana. His elder brother Pandit Kumar Bose, a celebrated Tabla Nawaaz of international repute, also gave him the fillip to venture into the world of music. He received vocal guidance from Sri Jayanta Bose, his second brother, a vocalist of Bishnupur Gharana. Also he has received vocal guidance from Pandit Rajan and Sajan Mishra and Pandit Vijay Kichlu.

Samir Dey is a talented young tabla player from Kolkata. He has received extensive talim from Pandit Samir Chatterjee. He has accompanied many well known musicians in India and abroad.
