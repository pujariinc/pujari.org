Health Fair 2002

Sunday July 28, 2002 at the Global Mall

Sponsored by

Telugu Association of Metro Atlanta (TAMA)

Cosponsored by

IPN (Indian Professional Network),  BAGA (Bengali Association of Greater Atlanta),  GTS (Georgia Tamil Sangham),  PUJARI,  GAMA (Greater Atlanta Malayalee Association) and RAKSHA

Over 400 people from all walks of life, age groups and ethnic groups utilized this opportunity to get free medical examinations and consultations with leading medical specialists on Sunday July 28, 2002 at the Global Mall.

This is the first time in the history of Indian Community in Atlanta that 40 specialists in the medical field donated their time and services to “Save A Life”. The combined expertise of the 40 specialists of over 800 years in one location is again a First.

People started queuing up from 7:00 AM for this event to avail the blood tests that were being conducted at a nominal fee. The organizers of the event had anticipated this fact and made arrangements to start the testing an hour earlier to accommodate the needs of the community. The organizers extended the blood testing to accommodate the people.

We would like to thank Dr. Sujatha Reddy, who helped organizing this event. Without Dr. Reddy this event would not have been possible.
