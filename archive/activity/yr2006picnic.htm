Picnic 2006

Like every year Pujari will be hosting the picnic where we would like you to join us for some family fun including outdoor activities. We have chosen Fort Yargo State Park as the venue this time. This location is about 50 mins from Atlanta off I-85.

Address is: 210 S. Broad Street Winder , GA 30680

As activities we will have : Kids Sports and Fun activities, Ladies Sports and Musical chair, Mens Putting competition, Tennis and Beach Volley.

Please click on the link below for driving directions:

From I-285 take 85N.Take GA-316 E toward LAWRENCEVILLE / ATHENS after about 11 miles. Merge onto GA-81 / CHARLES S FLOYD RD / LOGANVILLE HWY after about 21 miles. End at 210 S Broad St (Fort Yargo State Park).

We will also have delicious grilled food and lemonade.
