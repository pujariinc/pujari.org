Baishakhi 2006: Celebration of Bengali New Year

Pujari cordially invites you to Baishakhi 2006.
It will be a grand cultural evening to celebrate the Bengali New Year.

There will be a lively show of local talents on traditional Bengali songs, dances and drama.

Following are the highlights:

  • Collection of songs and dances by children
  • Rabindra sangeet by Jnanabrata Bhattacharyya
  • Musical repertoire - 'Gauri Prasanna Majumdar Smarane'
