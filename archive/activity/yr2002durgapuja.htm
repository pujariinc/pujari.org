Spot drawing
(Bo-se An-ko)
competition

 Age restriction: Age 9 and under (Group A) and 9 to 14 years (Group B).
Paper size: 1/4th of the art paper.
Paper color: White.
Subject: As you wish, no restriction.
How many can I submit? Only one drawing per participant.
Participant may draw as many as he/she wants within the specified duration but he/she can submit only one for competition.
Duration: 1 hour.
Date and time: 11 am on 12th Oct, 2002.
Venue: IACA building.
Requirements: Participant will need to bring their own drawing materials (pencil, sharpener, eraser, ruler, pen, ink, paint, brush, crayon, easel etc) whatever is required for his/her drawing. There is no restriction of the medium. We will provide the paper only and participant has to draw only on the paper we will provide.
Judgment: There will be a panel of judges. Judgment will be done considering age of the participant in mind. Judges will not know the name of the participant and their decision will be final.
Prize and certificate: Every participant will receive the Certificate of Accomplishment and there will be a few prizes for excellency.
Notes: All art works will be displayed in the IACA hall. Excellent drawings/paintings may not be released immediately. We may publish in our website.
Registration: Please contact us at the collection desk on October 12, 2002 for name registration.
