Baishakhi 2004: Celebration of Bengali New Year

Pujari cordially invites you to Baishakhi 2004 (Nababarsha).
It is a grand cultural evening to celebrate the Bengali New Year.

Here are the major attarctions for this show:
1) Music and dance by talents from Atlanta and Georgia
2) Humorous skit from the works of legendary Bhanu Bandyopadhyay (Koutook Naksa)
3) Professional vocal music (Bengali songs including Najrul Geeti) -
By Pritam and Prithwiraj Bhattacharjee *
Pritam and Prithwi are disciples of Pandit Jasraj and Ustad Zaakir Hussain respectively
Pritam teaches vocal and Prithwi teaches tabla at our local chapter of Pandit Jasraj School.
Come and witness these talented young brothers' show for Bengalis of Atlanta
