Annual meeting 2001

First year annual meeting was held on 26th Jan 2002, Saturday, from 3 pm to 6 pm.

Venue: Merchant Walk Library.
1315 Johnson Ferry Road, Marietta, GA 30068.
Ph: 770-509-2730.

Direction

Take Interstate 75 north to Exit  263 (formerly exit 112) – South Marietta Parkway. Turn right onto the South Marietta Parkway. Go through two traffic lights and exit north onto Hwy. 120 – Roswell Road. Turn right onto Roswell Road and travel approximately 4.5 miles to the first sign for Merchants Walk Shopping Center. Turn right into Merchants Walk Shopping Center. Bear to the right. You will see the library on the right after Media Play.

Agenda

  1. Adoption of minutes of the last meeting.

  2. General annual report by the President.
  3. Financial report by the Treasurer.
  4. Election of the Bard of Directors.
  5. Formation of Executive Committee.
  6. Fix membership fee for year 2002.
  7. Planning of Saraswati Puja
