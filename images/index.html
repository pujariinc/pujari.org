Our Mission

Pujari is a Bengali non-profit organization based in Atlanta, USA. Its primary objective is to promote Bengali culture and heritage throughout North America. In addition, it provides an opportunity for the children to learn about ancient cultural tradition of India through various literary and other socio-cultural events organized by Pujari.
